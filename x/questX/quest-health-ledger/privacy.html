Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 9, 2025

1. Introduction
Welcome to QUEST* Health Ledger ("App"). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information. By using the App, you agree to the terms described herein.

2. Data Collection and Storage
The App operates entirely on your device, storing all data locally. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All entries, including fasting periods, food logs, and weight records, remain solely on your device.

3. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with third-party services or analytics tools. As a result, no data is shared with external entities.

4. User Control and Data Management
You have full control over your data within the App. You can edit or delete your entries at any time. Since the data is stored locally, uninstalling the App will result in the loss of all stored information. We recommend regularly backing up your device to prevent accidental data loss.

5. Security
While we have implemented measures to ensure the App's security, the safety of your data also depends on your device's security. Please ensure your device is protected against unauthorized access.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the App. Your continued use of the App after modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by mail at QUEST* Health Ledger LLC, 329 Riverside Dr, Northampton, Massachusetts 01062.
